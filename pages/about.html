Hobbies

I enjoy a wide range of hobbies that keep me curious and engaged. Some of my favorite pastimes include:

  • Robotics: Collaborating with my robotics team to design and program innovative robots.
  • Photography: Capturing the beauty of the world through my lens, especially in nature and urban settings.
  • Building Computers: Assembling powerful machines tailored for specific purposes like gaming or development.
  • Hiking: Exploring scenic trails and enjoying the tranquility of the outdoors.
  • Astrophotography: Combining my love for photography and astronomy to capture stunning images of the night sky.

Programming

As a programming enthusiast, I have experience with several languages, but my primary focuses are:

  • C#: Ideal for game development and creating robust applications.

  • Python: A versatile tool for data analysis, automation, and scripting.

This role allows me to apply my skills to real-world challenges and collaborate with a talented group of peers. The experience has been both rewarding and instrumental in shaping my technical expertise and teamwork abilities.
